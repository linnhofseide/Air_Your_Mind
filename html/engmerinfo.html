Being outdoors is important

The reason why the app was created is that many are too much inside. We humans are animals, and like other animals we have a natural connection to nature and being outdoors - yes, although being indoors is usually most comfortable and convenient for us. If the app notices that you have been outdoors too little, it will therefore make you aware of this.

Timing is the thing

The creators of Air your mind know that you have a life and personal needs that need adaptation. Therefore, the most important app feature is to Air your mind that it takes into account your routines - both school, work and when you usually wake up. If you have a job on Tuesdays between 9-17, the app will not force you to take a trip out on Tuesdays between 9-17.

Comfort

Some other important features that the app has is that it is bilingual (Norwegian, English) and has different font sizes, line spacing and color themes (light, dark, color-blind-friendly). A comfortable user experience is something we place high on the priority list.
