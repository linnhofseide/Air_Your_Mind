Being outdoors is important

The reason why the app was created is that many of us are too much inside. As humans we are animals, and like other animals we have a natural connection to nature and being outdoors - yes, although being indoors is usually the most comfortable and convenient for us. If the app notices that you have been outdoors too little, it will make you aware of this.

Timing is the thing

The creators of Air your mind know that you have a life and personal needs that need adaptation. Therefore, the app's most important app feature is that it takes into account your routines - including school, work and when you usually wake up. If you let the app know that you have work on Tuesdays between 9-17, the app won't give you a notification telling you to take a trip out on a Tuesday between 9-17.

Comfort

Some other important features that the app has is that it is bilingual (Norwegian, English) and has different font sizes, line spacing and color themes (light, dark, color-blind-friendly). A comfortable user experience is something we place high on the priority list.
